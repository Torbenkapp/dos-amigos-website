Our Story: From a Trailer to Your Town

It all started with a simple flatbed trailer and a big dream. As two engineering students, we decided to combine our skills and passion for good food to create something special.

Every measurement was calculated, every beam was cut and welded by us. We designed the entire structure to be modular, a testament to our engineering roots.

After countless hours of hard work, we had our first day of sales in the heart of Horsens. The feeling of serving our first burrito was unforgettable!
